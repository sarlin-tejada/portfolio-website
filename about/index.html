Exploring Life Through Data
I’ve been on a journey that’s taken me through software engineering, real estate, caregiving, and deep personal transformation—and through it all, one constant has remained: a persistent pull toward understanding patterns. Whether in behavior, business, or life, I’ve always wanted to know the “why” behind what we do, when we do it, and how we can do it better.

Data has become my language for that exploration. It’s how I make sense of the past, stay grounded in the present, and design what comes next—with clarity, curiosity, and purpose. This space is where I share that work, one insight at a time.
